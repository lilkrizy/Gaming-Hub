EmailJS Setup Guide

To enable actual email sending in your ebook store, follow these steps:

Step 1: Create EmailJS Account

Go to https://www.emailjs.com/ and create a free account.

Step 2: Add Email Service

In your EmailJS dashboard:

  1. Go to "Email Services"
  2. Click "Add New Service"
  3. Choose your email provider (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  4. Connect your email account
  5. Note down your Service ID

Step 3: Create Email Template

In your EmailJS dashboard:

  1. Go to "Email Templates"
  2. Click "Create New Template"
  3. Use this template:
Subject: Your Ebook Download - {{book_title}} Dear Customer, Thank you for your purchase! Here are your download details: Book Title: {{book_title}} Download Link: {{download_link}} Click the link above to download your ebook. The link will remain active for 30 days. If you have any questions, please contact our support team. Best regards, Anive Edjour's Ebook Store

Note down your Template ID

Step 4: Get Your Public Key

In your EmailJS dashboard:

  1. Go to "Account" or "Integration"
  2. Find your Public Key

Step 5: Update sell.html

Replace the placeholder values in sell.html:

  • Replace "YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY" with your actual public key
  • Replace 'YOUR_SERVICE_ID' with your service ID
  • Replace 'YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID' with your template ID
Important Notes:
  • EmailJS has a free tier with limited emails per month
  • Test the email sending first with your own email
  • The download links are currently placeholder URLs - you'll need to host actual ebook files
  • For production use, consider a more robust email solution

Once configured, the ebook store will send real emails with download links to customers after successful payment!
