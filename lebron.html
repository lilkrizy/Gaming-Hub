LeBron James

Ranking: Number One

Biography

LeBron Raymone James (born December 30, 1984) is an American professional basketball player. He is widely considered one of the greatest basketball players in NBA history. LeBron was highly anticipated before entering the NBA and has lived up to the hype as a three-time NBA champion and four-time NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP).

Legacy

LeBron James has not only had a lasting impact on the court but also off the court. His philanthropic efforts include supporting various educational initiatives, and he has opened the "I PROMISE School" for at-risk children in his hometown of Akron, Ohio.
